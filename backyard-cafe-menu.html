Tucked away in Belgaum's heartbeat, The Backyard is where lush greenery meets bold flavours. Whether you're here for a morning brew, a wood-fired grill feast, or an evening under the stars with friends — this is your place to breathe.

We serve every plate with passion, pour every drink with purpose, and keep every corner wrapped in nature so you never want to leave.
